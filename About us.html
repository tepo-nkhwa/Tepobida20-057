History

Moscow is the capital city of Russia. The city of Moscow gradually grew around the Moscow Krelim, it was named after the river it is situated in called Moskva River. Throughout the city existence it has been lead by different mayors. The first mayor Gavriil Popov took office on the 12 of 1991, his succesor Yury Luzhkov took office on the 6 of June 1992, followed Vladimir Resin who assumed the position on the 28 of September 2010 as an acting mayor, Sergey Sobyanin then took office a month later on the 21 of October till date.
