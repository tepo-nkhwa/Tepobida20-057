Mercedes Benz Fashion week

Mercedes Benz signed its first Title sponsorship agreement with Russian Fashion Week in spring 2011. Mercedes Benz Fashion week Russia is the biggest and the most widely media covered fashion event in Russia. The event happens biyearly in spring and autumn and Moscow had the privilege to host the event from the 19 to the 21 of October 2020. 117 designers and 1150 were part of the of this 5 day fashion celebration. Futhermore, this large scale event was covered by more than 1600 Russian and foreign journalists and more than 850 influencers.

World Cup

The 2018 FIFA Mens world cup was held in Russia. The tournament contested of by men`s national teams and took place between 14 June and 15 July 2018 in Russia. The tournament phase involved 32 teams and 64 matches were played across Russia, Moscow hosting 7 matches using the Luzkhini Stadium and Saint Petersburg two of the biggeststadium in Russia.The final match of the tournament was played in Luzkhini Stadium with 79 865 fans showing up to watch France vs Croatia, with france ending up winning the match by by 4 goals to 2 of Croatia.
